📚 Grade 2 PBL Projects — The World That Never Goes Dark

🏙️ Grade 2 students explore electricity through an exciting multi-week project-based learning adventure! Driving Question: How can simple circuits help people when electricity is not available?

Week 1
🏙️

Team Formation & City Planning

Form your team! Discover what electricity does. Sort items: needs power / no power.

Week 2
⚡

Electricity Safety & Circuits

Learn safety rules! Build open and closed circuits. Make a lightbulb glow! ⚠️ Water + Electricity = DANGER!

Week 3
🏗️

Building the Electrified City

Connect circuits to buildings. Light up the hospital, police station and homes. Series circuits!

Week 4
🌆

The Giant Electrified City!

3… 2… 1… POWER UP! Present your electrified city and show circuits help people!

🪔

Electric Art Adventure — Lumi's Lamp

Meet Lumi! Design a lamp with colors and shapes. Decorate the shade, make the base strong, connect the wire!

🌃

City at Night Art Project

Paint the sky blue, cut black buildings, add yellow window squares — Let There Be Light!

🤖

Electric Math Adventure

Help the robot fix the school generator using counting, addition, subtraction, shapes & reflections!

💻

Electrical Story in Scratch

Be a Super Coder! Snap a photo of an electrical object, upload to Scratch, make your hero tell the story!

⚠️ Electrical Safety Rules!

🚫 Rule 1: Electricity + Water = DANGER! Keep them apart!
⚡ Rule 2: Never touch broken wires! Tell a grown-up!
🤚 Rule 3: Never touch plugs with wet hands!
